Idir Mohamed Yacine (ARMINES, postdoc WP4) has released his R package for efficient spatial modeling with Vecchia approximation, supporting Gaussian Processes (GP) and Spatially Varying Coefficients (SVC) models with optional censoring and Bayesian inference. [Available here](https://github.com/yacine-idir/VecchiaCensored-R-Package).
